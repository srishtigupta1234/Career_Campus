About Career Campus

Your companion in exploring colleges, unlocking potential, and simplifying admissions.

What is Career Campus?

Career Campus is a student-centric platform designed to guide learners through their educational journeys. Whether it's identifying the right college or understanding eligibility, our tools simplify it all with intelligence and care.

Why Choose Us?

  • Personalized guidance based on aptitude tests
  • Real-time eligibility matching with colleges
  • One-click scheme application & tracking
  • Admin & staff-based approval process
  • Fast and secure with Firebase backend

Behind the Code

Built with a vision to solve real problems in rural and urban student communities alike, this platform reflects the dedication of a first-year BCA student combining tech skills with real-world internship experience at Unified Mentor.

  • Designed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Firebase
  • Dynamic college and scheme data fetched live from Firestore
  • Modular architecture with student, admin, and staff dashboards
  • Interactive UI inspired by real government portals
  • Styled with smooth gradients, shadows, and responsive layout
